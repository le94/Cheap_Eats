Snacks

Popcorn

A nice and easy snack that takes less than five minutes to make and is the perfect compliment for your Netflix marathon.

Source: Gimme Some Oven's Popcorn

  1. You will need the following ingredients:
    • 1 cup of popcorn kernels
    • 1 tspn of salted butter
  2. Place a cup of popcorn kernels into a pot and turn the heat onto medium.
  3. Once the kernels have popped, pour the popcorn into a large bowl.
  4. Use the remaining 1 tspn of butter into a pan and heat the butter on medium-low.
  5. Once the butter has melted, drizzle the butter on top of the popcorn. Mix the popcorn well into the batch and enjoy!
Apple Slices with Peanut Butter

A healthy and filling snack that combines your crave of an apple and peanut butter.

Source: Laa Loosh's Peanut Butter Apple Slices

  1. You will need the following ingredients:
    • 1 apple
    • 1 jar of peanut butter
  2. Wash the apple well and peel the skin of the apple based on preferrence.
  3. Cut your apple into your preferred shape in size.
  4. Spread a generous amount of peanut butter based on your liking on each apple slice and enjoy!

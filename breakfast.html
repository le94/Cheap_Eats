Breakfast Sandwich

A quick easy sandwich including eggs, choice of meat, and cheese on top of a warm English muffin.

Source: Christopher Testani's BA's Best Breakfast Sandwich

  1. You will need the following ingredients:
    • 1 egg
    • 1 slice of ham or spam or sausage patty
    • 1 English muffin
    • 2 tsp of butter
    • cheese (optional)
  2. Cut the english muffin in half and spread 1 tspn of butter on both sides of the muffin.
  3. Toast the muffin for about 3 minutes (shorter or longer based on your preference). Once your muffin is done, place the muffin on to a clean plate.
  4. Use the remaining 1 tspn of butter into a pan and heat the butter on medium-low. Once the butter has melted, place the slice of your choice of meat into the pan and turn the heat up to medium.
  5. Wait 2 minutes to get a nice sear and then flip the slice of meat to the other side and wait for another 1 minute.
  6. Transfer the slice of meat to one side of the muffin and crack an egg into the pan. Add salt and pepper and fry the egg to your liking.
  7. Place your friend egg on top of the slice of meat. Sprinkle some of your cheese if you have any.
  8. Use the 2nd slice of muffin to close the sandwich and your sandwich is now ready to be devoured.
Avocado Toast

A slice of toasted multigrain topped with fresh avocado spread and a pinch of black pepper.

Source: skinnytaste's Avocado Toast with Sunny Side Egg

  1. You will need the following ingredients:
    • 1 slice of multigrain toast
    • 1 avocado
    • 1 lime
    • 1 tspn of butter
    • 2 tspn of cilantro
    • 1 egg
  2. Toast the slice of bread for about 3 minutes (shorter or longer depending on your preference). Once your toast is done, place it on to a clean plate.
  3. Cut the avocado in half (using your knife to cut around the elongated side) and scoop out the avocado flesh of one half into a bowl.
  4. Finely chop the cilantro and add your preferred amount into the bowl.
  5. Add the juice of a quarter of a lime and mix everything into a smooth or chunky consistency based on your liking.
  6. Sprinkle salt and pepper to your liking
  7. Spread the avocado mix on one side of the toast. To add an egg on your toast, keep reading. If not, enjoy!
  8. Put 1 tspn of butter into a pan and heat the butter on medium-low. Once the butter has melted, crack your egg into the pan and turn the heat up to medium. Fry the egg with salt and pepper to your liking (sunnyside up is recommended).
  9. Place your friend egg on top of the slice of the spread and enjoy!

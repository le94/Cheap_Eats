Pizza

A fun dish that lets you throw in all your favorite ingredients. More importantly, you can watch some Netflix while you let the oven do all the work.

Source: 360bistrobar's Pepperoni Pizza

  1. You will need the following ingredients:
    • 1 store bought pizza dough
    • 1 can of pizza sauce
    • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1 tbspn of olive oil
    • 1 bag of pepperoni slices and bits of sausage meat
  2. Preheat your oven between 400 to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Stretch the pizza dough until you make a circle of diameter 12 inches.
  4. Brush some olive oil on the circled dough.
  5. Sprinkle some cheese (about 1 inch) around the circumfrence of the dough.
  6. Pull the edges of the dough and wrap over the cheese to make a cheese crust.
  7. Spread a layer of pizza sauce on top of the dough.
  8. Sprinkle the mozerlla cheese on top of the sauce until no big red spots can be seen. Then, add a generous amount of pepperoni slices and bits of sausage meat on top of the cheese.
  9. Bake the pizza in the oven for about 20 minutes until your pizza is nice and golden.
  10. Cut your pizza into your preferred shape and enjoy!
Pesto Pasta with Cherry Tomatoes and Fresh Mozzarella

A filling meal that only takes 30 minutes to make and requires no meal preparation. Plus, it is easy to make in big batches for lunch or dinner for the next day.

Source: Three Olives Branch's Pesto Caprese Penne Pasta

  1. You will need the following ingredients:
    • 1 box of pasta
    • 1 jar of Pesto Sauce
    • 1 box of cherry tomatoes
    • 1 pack of shredded mozzarella cheese
  2. Boil 10 cups of water in a large pot. Once the water starts to rapidly boil, add a big pinch of salt into the water.
  3. Throw in your pasta and follow the cooking directions as stated on the box.
  4. Once the pasta has been cooked to your liking, drain the pasta. DO NOT rinse the pasta.
  5. Pour the pasta in your mixing bowl and scoop out enough pesto sauce to your liking. Stir until the pasta is nicely coated with the pesto sauce.
  6. Wash the cherry tomatoes and cut the tomatoes in half. Add the tomatoes into the mixing bowl and stir until eveything is well mixed.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella on top of the pasta and enjoy!

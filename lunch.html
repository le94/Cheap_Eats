Fried Rice

A nice medley of easy to find ingredients mixed with rice and a hint of soy sauce.

Source: Gimme Some Oven's Fried Rice

  1. You will need the following ingredients:
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 bowl of leftover rice
    • 1 tspn of cooking oil
    • 1 tspn of soysauce
    • 5 oz. of ground beef
    • 1/3 cup of frozen diced carrots
    • 1/3 cup of frozen snow peas
    • 1/3 cup of frozen corn
    • 1 green onion (optional)
  2. Microwave the leftover rice for 30 seconds.
  3. Turn on your stove to medium heat and add a tspn of cooking oil into your pan. Put in the ground beef and adding salt and pepper to your liking and stir fry for about 5 minutes until brown.
  4. Before the ground beef is fully cooked,throw in your frozen vegetables (frozen diced carrots, snow peas, and corn) and continue cooking. Stir fry for about 3 minutes until all the toppings are fully cooked.
  5. Add your rice and mix it all together. Add your soy sauce and stir until everything is blended.
  6. Transfer your fried rice into your bowl. Dice some green onions, if you have any, and sprinkle it on top and enjoy!
California Roll

A fancy dish that is easy to pack and has only six main ingredients.

Source: norecipes.com's California Roll

  1. You will need the following ingredients and tools:
    • 1 bowl of sushi rice
    • 1 tspn of rice vinegar
    • 1 pack of imitation crab
    • 1 cucumber
    • 1 avocado
    • 1 pack of seaweed sheets
    • 1 bamboo sushi rolling mat
  2. Cook the sushi rice based on the directions give on the package.
  3. Place the fresh, warm rice into a bowl. For every 5 cups of rice, add 1 tspn of the rice vinegar. Mix the rice and vinegar together. Add salt to your liking.
  4. Defrost the imitation crab and cut the imitation crab along the long side into two strips.
  5. Cut the avocado into thinly sliced pieces.
  6. Peel the cucumber and cut the cucumber into thin strips.
  7. Take your rolling mat and place a sheet of seaweed on top of the mat.
  8. Using a spoon, scoop some of your rice on top of the seaweed and spread the rice. The sheet should be fully covered with an even layer of rice.
  9. Place a strip of imitation crab, cucumber, and a slice of avocado on top of the rice on the edge that is closest towards you.
  10. Using your rolling mat, wrap the toppings by rolling the sheet of seaweed into a scroll. Gently squeeze the roll so that your sushi roll is compact.
  11. Use a sharp knife, cut the roll into your desired size and shape of a sushi piece.
